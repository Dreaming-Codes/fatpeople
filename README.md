Drinking **Prime Energy** isn't just about the rush; it's about the growth.
- **Dynamic Scaling**: As you consume more, your player model (and collision box!) grows wider. 
- **Levels of Power**: Reach Level 5 to become 2.5x wider than a standard player. You might not fit through doors, but you'll be a juggernaut on the field.

## üé≥ Human Bowling: The Rolling Mechanic
Once fueled by Prime Energy, your movement changes forever.
- **Initiate the Roll**: Jumping while under the influence has a high chance to trigger a forced **Roll**.
- **Unstoppable Momentum**: Once you're rolling, you move at high speeds, smashing through blocks and rotating forward with physical force.
- **The Bowling Ball**: Collide with any living entity to deal damage and send them flying with **extreme knockback**.
- **Physics-Defying Bounces**: Take no fall damage! Instead, you'll bounce off the ground or even skip across the surface of water.

Stopping isn't as easy as starting. 
- **Struggle Bar**: When rolling, a unique GUI appears. You must spam the **Jump** key to fight against the momentum and regain control.
- **High Stakes**: The higher you fall from, the harder it is to "struggle" out of the roll.
